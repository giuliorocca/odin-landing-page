Building Better Worlds
Weyland-Yutani is consistently portrayed as exhibiting the worst aspects of corporate profiteering, willing to sacrifice decency and life in the endless pursuit of monetary profits.
Divisions

Bio-Weapons Division

A special department that deals with the research and development of biological and viral weapons. One of its primary goals is to obtain a viable specimen of the species Xenomorph XX121, whether it be in the form of a Facehugger, a Chestburster, or a mature adult.

Reverse-Engineering Division

Run with little regard for morals, although owing to the research being based primarily on the study of appropriated technology, the scope for appalling breaches of human rights is less open. Even so, the division ruthlessly captures and exploits alien tech, stealing Predator and Engineer technology with little thought as to how the species that created it may react.

Special Services Division

Run by Carter Burke, who acts as Special Projects Director within the department. It manages various other aspects of the company's research programs. Further details available upon inquiry.

Conventional Weapons Division

Involved in the manufacture of conventional weaponry and is a major supplier for the United States Colonial Marine Corps. It produces various firearms as well as specialized ammunition for specific circumstances.
